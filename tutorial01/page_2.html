Concert Photography: Tips and Tricks

  1. Use a fast shutter speed
  2. Use a wide aperture
  3. Set your camera to rapid shooting and take as many photos as you can
  4. Bring extra batteries
  5. Get to the venue early

Camera Settings: A brief guide

Camera Setting Description
ISO Adjusts the brightness of an image. Higer ISOs will take in more light, however, the image will be grainier.
Shutter speed Adjusts how fast the camera takes the picture. Faster shutter speeds can take pictures of fast moving subjects, but need more light
Aperture Adjusts how much light the camera lets in. Wider apertures will be brighter, but less of the image will be in focus.
